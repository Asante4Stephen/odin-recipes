Jollof Rice

Description

Jollof rice is a popular West African dish made with rice, tomatoes, and spices. It is rich, flavorful, and often served at parties and special occasions.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Tomato paste
  • Onions
  • Pepper
  • Vegetable oil
  • Spices
  • Chicken or beef (optional)

Steps

  1. Blend tomatoes, onions, and pepper.
  2. Heat oil and fry the mixture.
  3. Add spices and tomato paste.
  4. Add rice and stir well.
  5. Add water and cook until rice is soft.
