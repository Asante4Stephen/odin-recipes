Fried Rice

Description

Fried rice is a simple and tasty dish made by stir-frying cooked rice with vegetables, eggs, and sometimes meat.

Ingredients

  • Cooked rice
  • Vegetables (carrots, peas)
  • Eggs
  • Soy sauce
  • Oil
  • Chicken or shrimp (optional)

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a pan.
  2. Scramble eggs and set aside.
  3. Cook vegetables.
  4. Add rice and stir well.
  5. Add soy sauce and mix everything together.
